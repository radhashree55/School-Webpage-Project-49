ABOUT- Blooming Dales International School

Blooming Dales International School was founded in year 2001 as a primary school Blooming Dales Montessori headed by the renowned educationist Mrs. Meetu Sharma with the strength of 70 students. Her vision encompasses not only children but parents, teachers and the society on the whole. It is only hard work and determination that it has now emerged as a unique Institution progressing towards unprecedented heights under the patronage of Bharti Charitable Trust since year 2009.
